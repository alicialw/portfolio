Sustainable YOU

Brand Extension

Album

NOPA x RMIT University Vietnam
Genre

Concept development
Graphic design
Motion design

Brief

A limited bottle collection, by students, for students

Idea

Single-use plastic remains a pressing environmental challenge among young professionals.

Sustainable YOU creates a character-driven experience with four distinct personas that transform routine beverage consumption into opportunities for environmental stewardship, personalizing sustainability.

COURSE: RMIT University Vietnam — Design Management & Collaborative Studio
DURATION: 3 months
