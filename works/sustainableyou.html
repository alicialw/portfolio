Track Notes

Single-use plastic remains one of the most pressing environmental challenges, especially among young professionals with on-the-go lifestyles. While alternatives exist, they often lack the personal connection and to inspire lasting behavior change.

Sustainable You addresses this challenge by creating character-driven experiences that transform routine beverage consumption into opportunities for environmental stewardship. By developing four distinct personas that resonate with different audience segments, we've personalized sustainability in ways that conventional environmental messaging cannot achieve.
