Idea

Paper receipts create ecological burdens, releasing harmful chemicals that pollute water sources.

Flow Without Receipts uses poetic storytelling to follow a paper boat—symbolizing a thermal receipt—as it navigates waterways and transforms into a sustainable e-receipt, creating a metaphor that inspires adoption.
