G'LAMS x MBNK 2023: Người tìm nơi sóng lặng

1st Phase Art Direction & Theatrical Projection

"Người Tìm Nơi Sóng Lặng" is a musical production collaboration by G'LAMS 2023 with Một Buổi Nhạc Kịch. This year, the production ventures into new territory, exploring a theme that is both challenging and relevant to younger generations: the psychological impact of war.

Through original songs, dances, and a compelling narrative, the musical offers a unique perspective on the post-war period. It has successfully captured the nuances of human emotions, presenting a heartfelt and thought-provoking exploration of a significant chapter in Vietnam's history.
