Track Notes

Paper receipts create a significant ecological burden, releasing harmful chemicals that pollute water sources and threaten environmental health. While digital alternatives exist, they lack compelling narratives that inspire widespread adoption.

Flow Without Receipts addresses this challenge through poetic storytelling that transforms an everyday environmental concern into an emotional journey. We create a metaphor that resonates beyond typical environmental messaging by following a paper boat—symbolizing a thermal receipt—as it navigates waterways and eventually transforms into a sustainable e-receipt.
